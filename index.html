Instructions to Employees

  • Use this form to provide or update the bank account(s) information that your net pay will be deposited into.
  • Complete the form accurately. Incorrect information could cause delays in processing.
  • Submit the completed and signed form by email to your Pay and Benefits Service Centre. For instructions and other options, see the Document Submission Guide on the Pay and Benefits webpage (link does not work).
  • Note: If you are a new hire/rehire, send your completed and signed form to your manager. This document is part of your hire package and will be processed with the other hiring package documents.
